Upon kill, will lock the enemy in place, as a large sphere expands out of their head, then explodes inwards, representing how they are not lobotomized.

## Lobotomization
###### Actual Effects
- Players can no longer sprint, attack, jump, or use enchancements. They cannot use chat, speak in SVC, or hear words.

- Players lose all of their memories across all timelines and worlds.
